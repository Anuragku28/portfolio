About Me

Marketer by craft.
Growth strategist by nature.

I started my career at a performance-first agency, Social Frontier, where I learned to manage budgets, read audiences, and drive measurable results across both B2B and B2C clients.

What sets me apart: I've built and owned the full funnel — not just campaigns, but strategy, content, landing pages, automation, and reporting. I operate like a one-person growth team that also knows how to lead one.
